My Playtesting Philosophy:

Uncover The Experience

My primary role as a playtester is to uncover the unique experience your game offers to its players.

Does it deliver on the vision you have in mind as the designer?

What already succeeds and is fun? What could be improved?

Find What Is True

It is my belief that there are truths within the creative world: some games just are more fun than others.

Players can feel when a game needed more time in the tank.

My ambition is to help you discover these truths within your own project so that your next step can be taken with confidence and purpose.

Art = Hard

While my first duty is to be honest with you about your project, I also recognize the challenges that come with engaging in the creative process.

Creativity is vulnerable and difficult.

And because I understand this on a personal level, I will always approach your project with empathy, kindness, and a shared commitment to helping you further your journey.
