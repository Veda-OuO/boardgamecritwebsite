    Refund Policy

    Last Updated: February 8, 2026

    Our Commitment to You

    Greg In Play is committed to providing high-quality playtesting services that help game designers improve their projects. Your satisfaction is my priority, and I strive to deliver valuable insights and feedback for every session.

    Refund Eligibility

    We offer refunds under the following circumstances:

    Before Work Begins

    • Full Refund: If you cancel your playtesting session before I begin reviewing your materials or conducting the playtest, you are eligible for a full refund.
    • Timeline: Cancellations must be requested within 24 hours of payment and before any work has commenced.

    After Work Begins

    • Service Quality Issues: If you are dissatisfied with the quality of the playtesting service provided, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your report. We will work with you to address your concerns.
    • Partial Refunds: In cases where work has been partially completed, a prorated refund may be offered based on the amount of work already performed.

    Non-Refundable Situations

    Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

    • If you disagree with the feedback or opinions provided (I can only offer my perspective. My service is to provide honest, constructive criticism as I see it.)
    • If you fail to provide the necessary materials (rulebook, game files, etc.) within a reasonable timeframe after payment
    • If you request a refund more than 7 days after receiving your completed report

    How to Request a Refund

    To request a refund, please follow these steps:

    1. Email us at greginplaystudios@gmail.com with "Refund Request" in the subject line
    2. Include your order/payment confirmation number
    3. Provide a brief explanation of your reason for requesting a refund
    4. We will respond to your request within 2-3 business days

    Refund Processing Time

    Once a refund is approved:

    • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
    • The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase
    • Depending on your financial institution, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account

    Satisfaction Guarantee

    I stand behind the quality of my work. If you feel that the playtesting service did not meet your expectations or provide value, I want to know. I am committed to working with you to resolve any issues and ensure you receive the insights you need to improve your game.

    Modifications to This Policy

    Greg In Play reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. I encourage you to review this policy periodically.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions about our refund policy, please don't hesitate to reach out:

    • Email: greginplay@gmail.com
    • Response Time: I typically respond within 24-48 hours

    Thank you for choosing Greg In Play for your playtesting needs. I appreciate your business and look forward to helping you create an amazing game!
